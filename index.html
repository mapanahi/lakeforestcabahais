A Community United in Diversity

The Bahá'í community of Lake Forest is part of a global effort to contribute to the advancement of society. We believe that humanity is one single race and that the time has come for its unification in one global society.

Our community is open to everyone. Whether you are seeking spiritual answers, looking to involve your children in moral education, or wanting to serve your local neighborhood, you are warmly welcomed here.
