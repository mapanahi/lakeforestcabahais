The Oneness of Humanity

We are the Bahá'ís of Lake Forest, California — members of the worldwide Bahá'í Faith. We invite you to explore the teachings of our Faith and join us in creating a better world, one heart, one family, one neighborhood at a time.

At the center of the Bahá'í Faith is the figure of Bahá'u'lláh. The central theme of His message is that humanity is one single race and that the day has come for its unification in one global society. We believe in one Creator, the essential oneness of all major world religions, and the elimination of all forms of prejudice.

Building Community Together

All over Orange County and across the globe, Bahá'ís are engaged in a process of community building open to all. You are warmly invited to participate.

Children's Classes

Classes that awaken the spiritual capacities of children, focusing on virtues like truthfulness, kindness, and justice.

Junior Youth

Groups for ages 11-14 that empower young people to navigate a crucial stage of life and channel their energy into service.

Study Circles

Small groups exploring Bahá'í writings in a participatory manner, building capacity to serve the community.

Devotionals

Gatherings for collective prayer and reflection, open to all faiths, fostering a spirit of love and fellowship.

Connect With Us

Whether you'd like to join a devotional, enroll a child in classes, or simply ask a question, we'd love to hear from you.
